What the default rule set checks

Required & placeholders

title must not be blank / #NA / N/A. title_category must be Movies or TV Shows.

DAR / Competitive

DAR titles need companies = Pristine Brand and brand_set with Pristine DAR Brands; others need Competitive View.

IMDb & Metacritic

imdb_id must be a ttNNNNNNN / IMDb title URL; metacritic must be a metacritic.com movie/tv URL.

Wikipedia

wikipedia_page must be en.wikipedia.org/wiki/… and the article should match the title.

URL managers

url_managers must reference the row’s social accounts; Unknown & Pristine Brand companies are skipped.

Extensible

Add / edit rules in the JSON box — the engine is data-driven per column and check type.
